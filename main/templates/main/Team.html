About Us

What we are? hear from us

Everyone likes to eat something new and healthy daily. But it is a dilemma to find something that matches their preferences. This predicament leaded to solutions that involved food recommendation systems.
   Taste Buddies: The Food Counsellor platform connects customers serving their multiple needs. Customers use our platform to search and discover what meal they should take. When the user is confused, the web application will propose a meal to the user based on their history of categories and meals selected. The web application can be used to explore food options filtering as per their dietary constrictions and requirements. Also our website recommend or suggest the meal according to users requirement: according to the cuisine, vegetarian, non-vegetarian, satvik, sugar free, vegan, high or low protein. When a user selects a meal the website will provide all the information about the meal. Website will guide the user in preparation of the meal, right form the time taken, ingredient used, quantity, steps by step making. With the help of Taste buddies the user will be able to decide their meals quickly.
